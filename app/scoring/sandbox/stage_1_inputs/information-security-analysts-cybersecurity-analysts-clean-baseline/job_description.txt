**Job Title:** Information Security Analysts / Cybersecurity Analysts

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking highly skilled Information Security Analysts/Cybersecurity Analysts to conduct stress testing on our scoring engine, ensuring its reliability and performance under extreme conditions.

**Responsibilities:**

* Conduct thorough analysis of the scoring engine's architecture and design
* Develop and execute comprehensive stress test scenarios to identify potential vulnerabilities and performance bottlenecks
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and ensure alignment with business objectives
* Design, implement, and execute testing protocols to simulate various attack vectors and scenarios
* Analyze test results, identify trends, and provide actionable recommendations for improvement
* Develop and maintain detailed test reports, including findings, recommendations, and mitigation strategies
* Stay up-to-date with emerging threats, technologies, and industry best practices

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Cybersecurity, or related field; Master's degree preferred
* 3+ years of experience in information security analysis, testing, or a related field
* Strong knowledge of threat modeling, vulnerability assessment, and penetration testing methodologies
* Experience with stress testing tools and techniques (e.g., Apache JMeter, NeoLoad)
* Familiarity with cloud-based infrastructure and containerization technologies (e.g., AWS, Docker)
* Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills

**Preferred Skills:**

* Experience with scoring engine development or similar complex systems
* Knowledge of programming languages (e.g., Python, Java) and scripting tools (e.g., Bash, PowerShell)
* Familiarity with DevOps practices and continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines
* Certification in information security or a related field (e.g., CISSP, CEH)

**What We Offer:**

* Competitive salary and benefits package
* Opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects and contribute to the development of our scoring engine
* Collaborative and dynamic work environment with a team of experienced professionals
