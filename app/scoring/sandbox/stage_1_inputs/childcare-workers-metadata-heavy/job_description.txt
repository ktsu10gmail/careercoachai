* Salary Range: $120,000 - $180,000 per year
* Work Mode: Full-time, on-site
* Location: San Francisco, CA (with occasional remote work)
* Employment Type: Permanent
* Target Start Date: 6 weeks from hire date
* Travel: Occasional travel to industry conferences and customer sites (up to 20% of the time)

* Security Clearance: Top Secret clearance required

**Job Description:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine to join our team of experts in evaluating the performance and reliability of our scoring engine. As a key member of our Quality Assurance team, you will be responsible for designing, executing, and analyzing stress tests to ensure the stability and scalability of our system.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute comprehensive stress tests on our scoring engine, including load testing, endurance testing, and performance benchmarking
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, product management, and operations, to identify and prioritize test cases
* Develop and maintain a thorough understanding of the system's architecture, components, and interactions
* Analyze test results, identify trends and patterns, and provide actionable recommendations for improvement
* Work closely with the development team to reproduce and resolve issues identified during testing
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies, applying this knowledge to improve our testing methodologies

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering
* In-depth knowledge of scoring engine architecture and components
* Strong understanding of software development life cycles and testing methodologies
* Proficiency in scripting languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with load testing tools, including Apache JMeter or similar
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to interpret complex data sets
* Strong communication and collaboration skills, with experience working with cross-functional teams
