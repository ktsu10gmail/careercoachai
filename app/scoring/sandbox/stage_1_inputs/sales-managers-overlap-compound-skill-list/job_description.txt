**Sales Manager - Stress Test**

We are seeking a highly skilled Sales Manager to lead our sales team in conducting stress tests on scoring engines. As a key member of our operations team, you will be responsible for designing and executing comprehensive testing protocols to ensure the reliability and performance of our scoring engine.

**Job Summary:**

As a Sales Manager - Stress Test, you will be responsible for developing and implementing testing strategies to identify potential bottlenecks in our scoring engine. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to design and execute stress tests, analyze results, and provide recommendations for improvement. This is an exciting opportunity to join a dynamic team and make a meaningful impact on the performance of our scoring engine.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute comprehensive testing protocols to identify potential bottlenecks in the scoring engine
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement testing strategies
* Analyze test results and provide recommendations for improvement
* Develop and maintain relationships with key stakeholders, including product management and engineering teams
* Identify and prioritize areas for testing and resource allocation
* Develop and manage testing plans, schedules, and budgets
* Communicate test results and recommendations to stakeholders

**Required Skills:**

Java, Python, C++, MATLAB, R, SQL, Tableau, Excel, JIRA, Asana, Trello, Slack, Zoom, Webex, GitHub, GitLab, Jenkins, Docker, Kubernetes, AWS, Azure, Google Cloud Platform, Scrum, Agile, Lean Six Sigma, Test-Driven Development (TDD), Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD), Load Testing, Stress Testing, Performance Testing, User Acceptance Testing (UAT), Requirements Gathering, Technical Writing, Communication, Project Management, Time Management, Adaptability, Problem-Solving, Analytical Thinking, Attention to Detail, Creativity.

**Nice to Have:**

* Experience with machine learning and artificial intelligence
* Knowledge of cloud-based technologies and DevOps practices
* Familiarity with containerization and orchestration tools
* Experience with testing frameworks and libraries
* Certification in Scrum Master or Agile Coach
