**Job Title:** Credit Analysts / Counselors

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking experienced Credit Analysts/Counselors to join our team, responsible for conducting thorough credit analysis and providing expert guidance to clients on financial matters. The successful candidate will have a strong understanding of credit scoring models, market trends, and regulatory requirements.

**Responsibilities:**

* Conduct in-depth credit analysis using various scoring engines, including FICO, VantageScore, and proprietary models
* Evaluate credit reports, credit histories, and other relevant data to determine creditworthiness
* Provide expert counsel to clients on credit management strategies, debt consolidation options, and financial planning
* Develop and maintain relationships with clients to understand their financial needs and provide tailored solutions
* Stay up-to-date with market trends, regulatory changes, and new scoring models to ensure accurate analysis and recommendations
* Collaborate with internal teams, including underwriting and risk management, to ensure seamless client experience
* Prepare and present credit reports, analysis, and recommendations to clients and stakeholders

**Required Skills:**

Java, Python, R, SQL, Excel, Tableau, Power BI, FICO Scorecard, VantageScore, proprietary scoring engines, Bloomberg Terminal, Thomson Reuters Eikon, FactSet, S&P Capital IQ, Credit Karma, Experian, TransUnion, Equifax, credit analysis software (e.g. CreditBuilder, CreditRisk), financial modeling tools (e.g. Excel, Financial Modeling Software), regulatory compliance knowledge (e.g. Dodd-Frank, TRID), excellent communication and interpersonal skills, ability to work in a fast-paced environment, strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

**Preferred Qualifications:**

* Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, or related field
* 2+ years of experience in credit analysis or financial services
* Professional certifications (e.g. CFA, CAIA) or designations (e.g. Certified Credit Analyst)
* Experience with data visualization tools and software

**What We Offer:**

* Competitive salary and bonus structure
* Comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement plan, and paid time off
* Opportunities for professional growth and development in a dynamic and growing company
* Collaborative and supportive work environment
