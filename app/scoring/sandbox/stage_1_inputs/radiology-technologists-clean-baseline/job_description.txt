**Job Title:** Radiology Technologist - Stress Test Scoring Engine

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Radiology Technologist to join our team as a Stress Test Scoring Engine. As a key member of our imaging department, you will be responsible for scoring and interpreting stress test images to ensure accurate diagnoses and patient care.

**Responsibilities:**

* Score and interpret stress test images (EKG, echocardiogram, etc.) according to established protocols
* Evaluate image quality and report any defects or artifacts that may impact diagnostic accuracy
* Collaborate with cardiologists and other healthcare professionals to ensure accurate diagnoses and treatment plans
* Maintain up-to-date knowledge of new imaging technologies and techniques
* Participate in quality control measures to ensure consistency and accuracy in scoring
* Develop and maintain relationships with referring physicians and other stakeholders to improve patient care

**Required Skills:**

* 2+ years of experience as a Radiology Technologist or related field
* Strong understanding of cardiac anatomy and physiology
* Proficiency in EKG interpretation and stress test analysis
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work accurately and efficiently in a fast-paced environment
* Basic life support certification (BLS) required

**Preferred Skills:**

* Experience with advanced imaging technologies (e.g. cardiac MRI, CT angiography)
* Knowledge of electronic medical records (EMRs) and ability to navigate them effectively
* Certification as a Registered Technologist (RT) or Registered Cardiac Sonographer (RCS)
* Bilingual or multilingual skills

**What We Offer:**

* Competitive salary and benefits package
* Opportunities for professional growth and development
* Collaborative and supportive work environment
