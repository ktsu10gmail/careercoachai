**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer - Domain Scope Trap

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to design, develop, and execute comprehensive stress testing scenarios for our domain scope trap feature. The successful candidate will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize test cases, develop test scripts, and analyze results to ensure the reliability and performance of our system.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop stress testing scenarios for domain scope trap, including identifying key performance indicators (KPIs) and developing test scripts to simulate various loads and workloads
* Collaborate with product management, engineering, and QA teams to understand requirements and identify areas for improvement
* Develop and execute test plans, including setting up test environments, running tests, and analyzing results
* Identify and report defects or issues found during testing, working closely with the development team to reproduce and resolve them
* Provide regular progress updates and status reports to stakeholders
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in stress testing and performance engineering
