Responsibilities:

* Maintain accurate and up-to-date financial records, including accounts payable, accounts receivable, payroll, and general ledger
* Process and reconcile transactions, including invoices, payments, and deposits
* Prepare and submit financial reports to management, including balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements
* Conduct basic audits of financial records and identify discrepancies or errors
* Assist with budgeting and forecasting processes
* Provide support for special projects, such as financial analysis and data analysis

Required Skills:

* High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree in accounting or related field preferred
* 1-2 years of experience in bookkeeping, accounting, or auditing
* Proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel, Word, and Access
* Familiarity with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) and financial software such as QuickBooks or Xero
* Strong organizational and time management skills

Preferred Skills:

* Bachelor's degree in accounting or related field
* Experience with cloud-based accounting software such as QuickBooks Online or Xero
* Certification in bookkeeping, accounting, or auditing, such as Certified Bookkeeper (CB) or Certified Public Accountant (CPA)
* Knowledge of financial analysis and data analysis tools, such as Excel pivot tables and financial modeling
