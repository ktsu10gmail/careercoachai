<h2>Electrician - Scoring Engine Stress Test</h2>

<p>Join our team of expert engineers as a Scoring Engine Stress Tester! As an integral part of our Quality Assurance team, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and performance of our scoring engine.</p>

<p><strong>Job Summary:</strong></p>

We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented individual to conduct stress tests on our scoring engine. The successful candidate will be responsible for simulating various scenarios to identify potential bottlenecks, optimize system performance, and ensure the overall quality of our product.

<p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p>

* Conduct thorough analysis of the scoring engine's behavior under different loads and conditions
* Develop and execute comprehensive stress test plans to identify performance bottlenecks
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to reproduce and resolve issues
* Provide detailed reports on test results, including recommendations for improvement
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to inform testing strategies

<p><strong>Required Skills:</p>

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in software testing, quality assurance, or a related field
* Strong understanding of computer systems, algorithms, and data structures
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with stress testing tools and frameworks (e.g. Apache JMeter, NeoLoad)
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
