Responsibilities:

* Maintain accurate and up-to-date records in a filing system, including both physical and electronic files
* Sort, categorize, and distribute incoming documents to designated departments or personnel
* Process and file outgoing documents, ensuring timely delivery to recipients
* Perform data entry tasks using computer software, such as document management systems and spreadsheets
* Provide support for special projects, including data extraction and preparation of reports
* Maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion

Required Skills:

* High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree preferred
* 1-2 years of experience in a filing or administrative role
* Proficiency in Microsoft Office software, including Word, Excel, and Outlook
* Familiarity with document management systems and database software
* Strong organizational and time management skills

Preferred Skills:

* Experience with electronic filing systems and document scanning technology
* Knowledge of record-keeping laws and regulations, such as HIPAA or FERPA
* Certification in records management or a related field (e.g. Certified Records Manager)
* Bilingualism or proficiency in multiple languages
