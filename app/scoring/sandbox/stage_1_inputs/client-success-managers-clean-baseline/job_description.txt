**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team of experts in evaluating the performance and reliability of our scoring engine. As a key member of our Quality Assurance team, you will be responsible for designing and executing comprehensive stress testing scenarios to identify potential bottlenecks and areas for improvement.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute stress tests on our scoring engine to evaluate its performance under various loads and conditions
* Develop and maintain test scripts and frameworks to ensure consistency and efficiency in testing
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering and product management, to identify and prioritize testing efforts
* Analyze test results and provide actionable insights to inform product development and optimization strategies
* Work closely with the QA team to develop and implement automated testing tools and frameworks
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to ensure our testing practices remain current and effective

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in stress testing, performance engineering, or a related field
* Strong understanding of software architecture and design patterns
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++
* Experience with test automation frameworks (e.g. JUnit, Pytest)
* Knowledge of cloud-based infrastructure and scalability principles

**Preferred Skills:**

* Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 5+ years of experience in stress testing, performance engineering, or a related field
* Experience with containerization technologies (e.g. Docker) and orchestration tools (e.g. Kubernetes)
* Familiarity with machine learning and artificial intelligence concepts
* Certification in test automation or software testing methodologies
