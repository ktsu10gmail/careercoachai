**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer - Domain Scope Trap

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Stress Test Engineer to join our team in conducting comprehensive stress testing for our domain scope trap solution. The successful candidate will be responsible for designing, executing, and analyzing stress tests to ensure the reliability, scalability, and performance of our system under various loads.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute stress tests to identify system bottlenecks and areas for improvement
* Develop and maintain test scripts and tools to simulate realistic user behavior and traffic patterns
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product management, engineering, and operations, to ensure alignment and effective testing
* Analyze test results, identify trends, and provide actionable recommendations for system optimization and improvement
* Develop and maintain documentation of test plans, test scripts, and test results
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in stress testing and performance engineering
