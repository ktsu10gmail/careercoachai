Heavy Equipment Operator - Excavator Operations

We are seeking a skilled and experienced Heavy Equipment Operator to join our team. As an Excavator Operator, you will be responsible for operating heavy equipment in various construction and maintenance projects.

Responsibilities:

* Operate excavators, backhoes, and other heavy equipment safely and efficiently
* Conduct pre-operational checks on equipment before each use
* Load and unload materials from the excavator bucket or bed
* Dig foundations, trenches, and other excavation sites as required
* Assist in the placement of concrete, asphalt, and other materials
* Maintain a clean and organized work area
* Follow all safety protocols and regulations

Required Skills:

* Valid Heavy Equipment Operator certification (e.g. OSHA 30-Hour or equivalent)
* 2+ years of experience operating heavy equipment in construction or maintenance settings
* Strong knowledge of excavator operation, safety procedures, and maintenance requirements
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
* Excellent communication and problem-solving skills

Preferred Skills:

* Experience with hydraulic excavators (e.g. Caterpillar, Komatsu)
* Familiarity with GPS-guided excavation systems
* Certification in First Aid or CPR
* High school diploma or equivalent required
