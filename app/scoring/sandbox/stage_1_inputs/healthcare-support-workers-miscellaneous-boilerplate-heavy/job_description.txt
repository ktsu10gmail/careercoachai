**Job Title: Healthcare Support Worker (Miscellaneous)**

We're seeking highly skilled and compassionate Healthcare Support Workers to join our team! As a Healthcare Support Worker, you'll play a vital role in delivering exceptional patient care and support services.

**Responsibilities**

* Provide high-quality patient care and support services
* Collaborate with healthcare teams to develop and implement individualized care plans
* Conduct routine assessments and monitor patient progress
* Administer medications and treatments as directed by healthcare professionals
* Maintain accurate records and reports
* Participate in quality improvement initiatives and contribute to a culture of safety

**Required Skills**

* High school diploma or equivalent required; post-secondary education an asset
* Current certification in medical terminology, phlebotomy, or other relevant field (or willingness to obtain)
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision
* Basic life support certification (BLS) required; advanced certifications an asset
* Strong organizational and time management skills
* Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information
