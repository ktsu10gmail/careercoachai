Job Title: Stress Test Engineer

Job Summary:

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team. As a Stress Test Engineer, you will be responsible for designing and executing stress tests on our scoring engine to ensure its reliability, scalability, and performance under various loads.

Responsibilities:

* Design and execute stress tests on the scoring engine to identify bottlenecks and areas for improvement
* Develop and maintain test plans, scripts, and tools to simulate real-world workloads
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand system requirements and identify testing opportunities
* Analyze test results, identify trends, and provide recommendations for optimization
* Work closely with development team to reproduce issues and implement fixes
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to improve testing methodologies

Required Skills:

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in stress testing, performance engineering, or a related field
* Strong understanding of software development life cycles and testing methodologies
* Experience with load testing tools such as Apache JMeter, Gatling, or Locust
* Familiarity with cloud-based infrastructure and containerization technologies (e.g. Docker, Kubernetes)
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills

Preferred Skills:

* Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* Experience with programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++
* Knowledge of database performance optimization techniques
* Certification in stress testing or performance engineering (e.g. ISTQB, PTE)
* Experience with continuous integration and delivery tools (e.g. Jenkins, GitLab CI/CD)
