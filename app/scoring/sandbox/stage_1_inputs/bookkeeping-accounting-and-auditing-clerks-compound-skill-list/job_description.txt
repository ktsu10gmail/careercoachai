**Job Title:** Scoring Engine Stress Test Specialist

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Scoring Engine Stress Test Specialist to join our team. As a key member of our quality assurance team, you will be responsible for designing and executing comprehensive stress tests on our scoring engine to ensure its reliability, scalability, and performance under heavy loads.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute stress tests on the scoring engine to identify bottlenecks and areas for improvement
* Develop and maintain test plans, scripts, and tools to simulate various scenarios and workloads
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and ensure test coverage
* Analyze test results, identify issues, and provide recommendations for improvements
* Work closely with development team to implement fixes and enhancements based on test findings
* Develop and maintain documentation of testing processes, procedures, and results

**Required Skills:**

Java, Python, C++, JavaScript, SQL, Apache Kafka, Apache Cassandra, Docker, Kubernetes, AWS CloudFormation, Ansible, Selenium, JMeter, Gatling, Apache Spark, Apache Flink, PostgreSQL, MySQL, Redis, Git, Agile methodologies (Scrum or Kanban), Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, Excellent communication and collaboration skills, Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities, Experience with stress testing tools and techniques, Familiarity with cloud-based infrastructure and containerization.

**Nice to Have:**

Experience with machine learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch) or natural language processing libraries (NLTK, spaCy), Knowledge of DevOps practices and continuous integration/continuous deployment pipelines, Familiarity with container orchestration tools (Kubernetes, Docker Swarm), Experience with cloud-based testing platforms (AWS Cloud9, Google Cloud Test Lab).

**What We Offer:**

Competitive salary and benefits package, Opportunities for professional growth and development, Collaborative and dynamic work environment, Flexible working hours and remote work options.

If you are a motivated and detail-oriented individual with a passion for stress testing and quality assurance, please submit your application.
