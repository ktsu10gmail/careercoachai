Responsibilities:

- Prepare patients for exams and procedures by collecting medical history, vital signs, and other necessary information
- Conduct basic lab tests such as taking blood samples and processing specimens
- Maintain accurate and up-to-date patient records and medical histories
- Assist healthcare professionals with administrative tasks, such as scheduling appointments and preparing medical records
- Perform various clinical tasks, including ECGs, blood pressure checks, and vital sign monitoring
- Clean and maintain exam rooms, equipment, and other facilities to ensure a safe and hygienic environment

Required Skills:

- High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree in medical assisting preferred
- Completion of a state-approved medical assistant program
- Current certification as a medical assistant (CMA) or registered medical assistant (RMA)
- Basic life support (BLS) certification
- Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and stand for long periods

Preferred Skills:

- Experience working in a healthcare setting, preferably as a medical assistant
- Familiarity with electronic medical records (EMRs) systems
- Knowledge of medical terminology and anatomy
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills
