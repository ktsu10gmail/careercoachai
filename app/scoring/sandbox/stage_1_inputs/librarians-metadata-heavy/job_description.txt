**Job Description:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine to join our team of software engineers. As a Stress Test Engineer, you will be responsible for designing and executing stress tests on our scoring engine to ensure it can handle high volumes of metadata-heavy workloads.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute comprehensive stress tests on the scoring engine to identify performance bottlenecks
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, product management, and operations, to understand requirements and develop test plans
* Develop and maintain a suite of automated testing tools to support stress testing efforts
* Analyze test results and provide recommendations for improving the performance and scalability of the scoring engine
* Work closely with the development team to identify and prioritize areas for improvement
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies that may impact our scoring engine's performance

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering
* In-depth knowledge of software architecture, scalability, and performance optimization
* Experience with cloud-based infrastructure and containerization (e.g. Docker)
* Strong understanding of metadata storage and retrieval technologies (e.g. relational databases, NoSQL databases)
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with testing frameworks and tools (e.g. JMeter, Gatling)
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
* Strong communication and collaboration skills
