**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team of experts in evaluating the performance and reliability of complex systems. As a Stress Test Engineer, you will design, implement, and execute stress tests to identify potential weaknesses and vulnerabilities in our scoring engine.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop comprehensive stress test plans to simulate various types of loads and scenarios
* Execute stress tests on the scoring engine, monitoring performance metrics and identifying areas for improvement
* Analyze test results, providing detailed reports and recommendations for optimization and enhancement
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, product management, and operations, to ensure alignment and effective communication
* Develop and maintain a thorough understanding of the scoring engine's architecture, components, and interactions
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in stress testing and performance evaluation

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in stress testing, performance evaluation, or a related field
* Strong understanding of software architecture, design patterns, and system behavior
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with load testing tools and frameworks (e.g., Apache JMeter, Gatling)
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with attention to detail and ability to identify complex issues

**Preferred Skills:**

* Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 5+ years of experience in stress testing, performance evaluation, or a related field
* Experience with cloud-based systems, containerization (e.g., Docker), and microservices architecture
* Knowledge of DevOps practices, continuous integration, and continuous deployment
* Certification in stress testing or performance evaluation (e.g., ISTQB, CSTE)
