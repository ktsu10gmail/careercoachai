**Job Attributes:**

* Work Mode: Full-time, on-site
* Location: San Francisco, CA (with occasional travel to New York and London)
* Employment Type: Permanent
* Target Start Date: 6 weeks from hire date

**Job Description:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine to join our team of software engineers. As a key member of our engineering team, you will be responsible for designing and executing stress tests on our scoring engine to ensure it can handle large volumes of metadata-heavy workloads.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute comprehensive stress tests on the scoring engine to identify performance bottlenecks
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product management, engineering, and operations, to understand requirements and develop test plans
* Develop and maintain a suite of automated testing tools to support stress testing efforts
* Analyze test results and provide actionable recommendations for improving the scoring engine's performance and scalability
* Work closely with QA engineers to ensure that test cases are comprehensive and effective
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies, and apply this knowledge to improve our testing methodologies

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering
* In-depth knowledge of scoring engines and metadata-heavy workloads
* Strong understanding of software architecture and design patterns
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++
* Experience with automated testing frameworks and tools (e.g. JMeter, Gatling)
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
* Strong communication and collaboration skills

**Nice to Have:**

* Experience with cloud-based infrastructure and containerization technologies (e.g. AWS, Docker)
* Knowledge of machine learning or artificial intelligence concepts
* Certification in stress testing or performance engineering (e.g. ISTQB)
