The successful candidate will be responsible for designing, executing, and analyzing stress tests to identify performance bottlenecks and ensure the reliability of our systems.

* Design and execute stress tests to simulate various types of traffic and loads on our software applications
* Identify and analyze performance bottlenecks and areas for improvement
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including development, operations, and quality assurance, to understand system requirements and identify testing opportunities
* Develop and maintain test scripts, tools, and methodologies to ensure efficient and effective stress testing
* Analyze test results, identify trends, and provide recommendations for process improvements
* Communicate test findings and recommendations to stakeholders, including product management, development, and operations teams
