**Payroll and Timekeeping Clerks**

We are seeking highly organized and detail-oriented individuals to join our team as Payroll and Timekeeping Clerks. As a key member of our payroll and timekeeping department, you will play a critical role in ensuring accurate and timely payment of employee wages.

**Responsibilities:**

* Process payroll for all employees, including calculating salaries, benefits, and deductions
* Maintain accurate records of employee hours worked, including tracking overtime and leave time
* Ensure compliance with company policies and applicable laws and regulations, such as tax laws and labor laws
* Prepare and distribute paychecks to employees in a timely manner
* Reconcile payroll discrepancies and investigate any errors or irregularities
* Provide exceptional customer service to employees and management regarding payroll-related inquiries

**Required Skills:**

* High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree preferred
* 1-2 years of experience in payroll or timekeeping, preferably in a corporate setting
* Proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel and Word
* Strong organizational and attention-to-detail skills
* Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information

**Preferred Skills:**

* Experience with payroll software, such as ADP or Paychex
* Knowledge of tax laws and regulations, including federal, state, and local requirements
* Certification in payroll processing, such as Certified Payroll Professional (CPP)
* Bilingualism in English and Spanish preferred
