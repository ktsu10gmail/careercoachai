**About Us:**
SkyTech Aviation Services is a leading provider of aircraft maintenance and repair services. We are seeking an experienced Aircraft Mechanic to join our team for a stress test position.

**Job Summary:**
We are looking for a highly skilled and detail-oriented Aircraft Mechanic to perform comprehensive stress tests on aircraft engines. The successful candidate will be responsible for simulating extreme operating conditions to identify potential issues and ensure the reliability of our aircraft engines.

**Responsibilities:**

* Conduct thorough inspections and testing of aircraft engines under various operating conditions
* Develop and execute test plans to simulate extreme temperatures, pressures, and vibrations
* Monitor engine performance and make adjustments as needed to achieve optimal results
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and address potential issues
* Maintain accurate records of test results and recommendations for improvement

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience as an Aircraft Mechanic or in a related field
* Strong understanding of aircraft engine systems, including fuel systems, cooling systems, and electrical systems
* Experience with stress testing and simulation techniques
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
