**Job Description:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine to join our team. As a key member of our engineering organization, you will be responsible for designing and executing comprehensive stress tests on our scoring engine to ensure its reliability, scalability, and performance.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute stress tests on the scoring engine to identify bottlenecks and areas for improvement
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product management, engineering, and operations, to understand business requirements and develop test plans
* Develop and maintain a comprehensive testing framework to ensure thorough coverage of the scoring engine's functionality
* Analyze test results, identify trends, and provide recommendations for process improvements
* Work closely with the development team to implement changes and validate fixes
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to continuously improve our testing methodologies

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering
* Strong understanding of software architecture, scalability, and reliability principles

* Strong communication and collaboration skills
