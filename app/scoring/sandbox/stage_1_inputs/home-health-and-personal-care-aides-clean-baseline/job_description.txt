Job Title: Stress Test Engineer

Job Type: Full-time

Location: [Insert Location]

Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team. As a Stress Test Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and executing stress tests on our scoring engine to ensure its reliability, scalability, and performance.

Responsibilities:

* Design and develop comprehensive stress test plans to identify and prioritize areas of improvement
* Execute stress tests on the scoring engine, including load testing, endurance testing, and performance benchmarking
* Analyze test results and provide detailed reports on findings and recommendations for improvement
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, product management, and quality assurance to ensure alignment and effective communication
* Develop and maintain test scripts, tools, and infrastructure to support stress testing efforts
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to identify potential vulnerabilities and areas for improvement

Required Skills:

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in software testing, quality assurance, or a related field
* Strong understanding of computer systems, algorithms, and data structures
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++
* Experience with stress testing tools and frameworks, such as Apache JMeter or Gatling
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
* Strong communication and collaboration skills

Preferred Skills:

* Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 5+ years of experience in software testing, quality assurance, or a related field
* Experience with cloud-based technologies, such as AWS or Azure
* Knowledge of machine learning and artificial intelligence concepts
* Certification in stress testing or performance engineering (e.g. ISTQB, CSTE)
