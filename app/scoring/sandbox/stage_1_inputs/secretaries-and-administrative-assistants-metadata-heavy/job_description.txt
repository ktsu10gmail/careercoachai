**Job Attributes:**

* Salary Range: $120,000 - $180,000 per year
* Work Mode: Full-time, on-site
* Location: San Francisco, CA (with occasional remote work)
* Employment Type: Permanent
* Target Start Date: 6 weeks from posting date
* Travel: Occasional travel to industry conferences and customer sites (up to 20% of the time)

**Job Description:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine to join our team of experts in evaluating the performance and reliability of complex scoring engines. As a key member of our Quality Assurance team, you will be responsible for designing, executing, and analyzing stress tests to identify potential bottlenecks and areas for improvement in our scoring engine.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute comprehensive stress tests on our scoring engine, including load testing, performance benchmarking, and reliability analysis
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, product management, and operations, to understand system requirements and identify test scenarios
* Develop and maintain a thorough understanding of the scoring engine's architecture, components, and interactions
* Analyze test results, identify trends and patterns, and provide actionable recommendations for improvement
* Work closely with QA engineers to develop and execute automated testing scripts and tools
* Participate in code reviews and contribute to the development of new features and enhancements
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies, applying this knowledge to improve our scoring engine's performance and reliability

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering, preferably in a metadata-heavy environment
* In-depth knowledge of scoring engines, including data processing, caching, and query optimization
* Strong understanding of computer systems, networking protocols, and database architectures
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with load testing tools, such as Apache JMeter or Gatling
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to identify complex issues and develop effective solutions
* Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams
