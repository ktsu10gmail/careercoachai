Responsibilities:

- Conduct in-depth credit analysis on commercial and industrial loans to assess creditworthiness of potential borrowers
- Evaluate loan applications based on financial statements, industry trends, and other relevant factors
- Provide credit counseling and recommendations to clients, including suggestions for loan modifications or refinancing options
- Maintain accurate records of client interactions, loan applications, and credit reports
- Stay up-to-date with changes in lending regulations and industry standards
- Collaborate with internal stakeholders, such as underwriters and risk managers, to ensure consistency and accuracy in credit decisions

Required Skills:

- Bachelor's degree in finance, economics, or related field; 2+ years of experience in credit analysis or a related field
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with ability to interpret complex financial data
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with ability to work effectively with clients and internal stakeholders
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel, Word, and PowerPoint
- Experience with credit reporting software and databases

Preferred Skills:

- Master's degree in finance or related field; 5+ years of experience in credit analysis or a related field
- Certification as a Certified Credit Analyst (CCA) or Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA)
- Experience with loan origination systems, such as LoanGenius or LenderLogic
- Knowledge of industry-specific lending regulations and standards
