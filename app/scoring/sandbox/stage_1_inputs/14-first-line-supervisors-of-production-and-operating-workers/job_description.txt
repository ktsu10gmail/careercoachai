Responsibilities:

- Oversee daily production operations to ensure efficient use of resources and meet productivity targets
- Conduct regular inspections of production areas to identify and address quality control issues, safety hazards, and equipment malfunctions
- Provide guidance and training to junior production workers on new processes, procedures, and equipment operation
- Manage inventory levels and order supplies as needed to minimize downtime and optimize production flow
- Collaborate with maintenance personnel to schedule repairs and maintenance of equipment and machinery

Required Skills:

- High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree in industrial technology, manufacturing, or related field preferred
- 2+ years of experience as a production worker or supervisor in a manufacturing environment
- Strong knowledge of production processes, quality control procedures, and safety protocols
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to effectively interact with production workers, maintenance personnel, and other stakeholders
- Ability to analyze data and make informed decisions to optimize production operations

Preferred Skills:

- Bachelor's degree in industrial technology, manufacturing, or related field
- Experience with lean manufacturing principles, Six Sigma methodologies, or other quality improvement initiatives
- Familiarity with computerized maintenance management systems (CMMS) or enterprise resource planning (ERP) software
- Certification as a certified production supervisor (CPS) or equivalent credential
- Bilingual or multilingual skills to communicate effectively with diverse workforce
