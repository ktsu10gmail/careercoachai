* Meet monthly sales targets by identifying and pursuing new business opportunities with existing and prospective clients

* Valid insurance license (Life, Health, Property/Casualty)
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Preferred Skills:

* Experience with agency management systems (AMS) such as AgencyMate or Insurity
* Knowledge of insurance products, including life, health, property/casualty, and specialty lines
* Familiarity with data analysis tools (e.g. Excel, Tableau)
* Bilingualism in a second language (Spanish, French, etc.)
* Experience working in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities and deadlines
