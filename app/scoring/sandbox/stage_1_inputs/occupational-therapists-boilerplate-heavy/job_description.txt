**Job Title: Occupational Therapists**

We're seeking highly skilled and compassionate Occupational Therapists to join our team!

**Responsibilities**

* Conduct comprehensive assessments and develop individualized treatment plans for patients with various physical, cognitive, or mental health needs
* Implement evidence-based interventions and therapies to promote functional ability, mobility, and independence
* Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to coordinate care and ensure seamless transitions
* Develop and maintain relationships with patients, families, and healthcare providers to foster open communication and trust
* Stay current with industry developments and best practices through ongoing education and professional development

**Required Skills**

* Master's degree in Occupational Therapy from an accredited program
* Current licensure as an Occupational Therapist in the state of employment
* 2+ years of experience working with diverse patient populations, including children, adults, and older adults
* Strong assessment and treatment planning skills, with ability to adapt to changing patient needs
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with ability to work effectively with multidisciplinary teams
* Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion
