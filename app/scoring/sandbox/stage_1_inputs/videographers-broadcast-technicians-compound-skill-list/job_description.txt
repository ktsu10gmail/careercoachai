**Videographers/Broadcast Technicians - Stress Test**

We are seeking highly skilled videographers/broadcast technicians to participate in a comprehensive stress test designed to evaluate the performance of our scoring engine under extreme conditions.

**Job Summary:**

As a Videographer/Broadcast Technician, you will be responsible for operating and maintaining our scoring engine equipment while simulating various scenarios to identify potential bottlenecks and areas for improvement. This is an excellent opportunity to contribute to the development of cutting-edge technology and help shape the future of our platform.

**Responsibilities:**

* Operate and maintain the scoring engine equipment, including cameras, lighting, and audio systems
* Simulate different scenarios, such as high-speed video playback, multiple camera angles, and complex graphics overlays
* Monitor system performance and identify potential issues or bottlenecks
* Collaborate with our development team to provide feedback on system performance and suggest improvements
* Participate in regular stress testing sessions, which may include extended periods of operation without rest
* Maintain accurate records of test results and system performance

**Required Skills:**

Java, C++, Python, MATLAB, Adobe Premiere Pro, Avid Media Composer, Blackmagic Design DaVinci Resolve, Autodesk Maya, 3ds Max, Blender, Autodesk AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Windows 10, macOS High Sierra, Linux Ubuntu, Docker, Kubernetes, AWS CloudFormation, Azure DevOps, Git, SVN, Perforce, JIRA, Trello, Asana, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Webex, Google Meet, Skype, Adobe Creative Cloud, Autodesk Vantage Point, Blackmagic Design Intensity Pro

**Additional Requirements:**

* 2+ years of experience in video production or broadcast technology
* Strong understanding of computer systems and networking protocols
* Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
* Willingness to participate in regular stress testing sessions and provide feedback

**What We Offer:**

* Competitive hourly rate
* Opportunity to contribute to the development of cutting-edge technology
* Collaborative and dynamic work environment
* Professional development opportunities
