Retail Salesperson

We are seeking a highly motivated and customer-focused individual to join our retail team as a Salesperson. As a Salesperson, you will be responsible for providing exceptional customer service, driving sales growth, and maintaining a clean and organized store environment.

Responsibilities:

* Provide excellent customer service, respond to customer inquiries, and resolve issues in a professional manner
* Meet or exceed sales targets through effective product knowledge, upselling, and cross-selling techniques
* Maintain a clean and organized store environment, including visual merchandising and inventory management
* Process transactions accurately and efficiently, handling cash, credit card transactions, and returns
* Collaborate with colleagues to achieve team goals and objectives
* Participate in visual merchandising, window displays, and other promotional activities

Required Skills:

* High school diploma or equivalent required; some college education preferred
* 1-2 years of retail experience; sales experience an asset
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing situations
* Basic math skills and accuracy with handling cash and credit transactions

Preferred Skills:

* Previous experience in a similar retail environment or related field
* Knowledge of visual merchandising principles and techniques
* Familiarity with point-of-sale systems and inventory management software
* Bilingualism or multilingualism an asset
