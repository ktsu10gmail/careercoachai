**Job Title: Automotive Service Technicians**

We're seeking skilled and motivated Automotive Service Technicians to join our team! As a key member of our service department, you'll be responsible for performing routine maintenance, repairs, and diagnostics on a wide range of vehicles. If you have a passion for cars and enjoy working with your hands, we want to hear from you!

**Responsibilities**

* Perform routine maintenance tasks, such as oil changes, tire rotations, and brake pad replacements
* Diagnose and repair complex problems with vehicles, including electrical systems, engine issues, and transmission malfunctions
* Conduct inspections and assessments to identify potential problems before they become major issues
* Collaborate with other technicians and service advisors to ensure seamless customer experiences
* Maintain accurate records of repairs, maintenance, and diagnostics
* Stay up-to-date on the latest technologies, techniques, and industry developments

**Required Skills**

* 2+ years of experience as an automotive service technician or equivalent
* ASE certification in multiple areas (e.g., brakes, electrical systems, engine repair)
* Strong diagnostic skills and ability to troubleshoot complex problems
* Excellent communication and customer service skills
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
* Basic math skills and attention to detail
