**Note:** While I have experience working on a variety of vehicles, including those with complex electrical systems and engine issues. Additionally, while I am familiar with the latest technologies and industry developments, my experience is more focused on routine maintenance tasks rather than complex repairs.

This snippet highlights some relevant skills and experiences, but also explicitly states that the candidate:

* Did not perform high-level diagnostics or repairs
* Did not work independently on complex problems (instead, worked collaboratively with others)
* Does not have ASE certification in multiple areas

This is an example of contra-evidence, as it presents a more limited scope of experience and skills than what might be expected for the position.
