**Job Title:** Computer Systems Analysts

**Job Attributes:**

* Salary Range: $85,000 - $125,000 per year
* Work Mode: Full-time
* Location: Remote (with occasional on-site visits at our headquarters in San Francisco, CA)
* Employment Type: Permanent
* Target Start Date: 6 weeks from application date
* Travel: Occasional travel to industry conferences and training events (up to 10% of work time)
* Sponsorship: We offer sponsorship for international candidates who meet the necessary requirements
* Security Clearance: Not required, but a valid US government security clearance may be required for certain projects

**Job Description:**

We are seeking highly skilled Computer Systems Analysts to join our team in designing and implementing stress testing protocols for our metadata-heavy applications. As a key member of our Quality Assurance team, you will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize testing scenarios that simulate real-world usage patterns.

Responsibilities:

* Design and execute comprehensive stress tests on our metadata-heavy applications, including performance, scalability, and reliability testing
* Collaborate with development teams to gather requirements and ensure test plans align with business objectives
* Develop and maintain test scripts, tools, and frameworks to automate testing processes
* Analyze test results, identify trends, and provide actionable recommendations for process improvements
* Work closely with QA engineers to develop and implement automated testing frameworks
* Participate in code reviews and contribute to the improvement of our testing infrastructure
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies, applying knowledge to improve testing methodologies

Required Skills:

* 5+ years of experience in software testing or a related field (e.g., QA engineering, DevOps)
* Strong understanding of computer systems, networking protocols, and database architectures
* Experience with stress testing tools such as Apache JMeter, Gatling, or Locust
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to identify complex issues and develop creative solutions
* Strong communication and collaboration skills, with experience working with cross-functional teams
* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field
