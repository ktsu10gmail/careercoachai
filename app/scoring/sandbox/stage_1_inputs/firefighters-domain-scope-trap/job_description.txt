**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer - Domain Scope Trap

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to design and execute comprehensive stress testing scenarios for our software applications, with a focus on identifying potential performance bottlenecks in our domain scope trap feature.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop realistic stress test scenarios to simulate high-traffic, high-stakes user interactions
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify key performance indicators (KPIs) and testing objectives
* Execute stress tests using various tools and techniques, including load testing, endurance testing, and performance benchmarking
* Analyze test results, identifying trends, patterns, and areas for improvement
* Develop and maintain detailed test plans, scripts, and reports to document testing activities and outcomes
* Work closely with QA teams to ensure seamless integration of stress testing into the overall quality assurance process

**Required Skills:**

* 3+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering
* In-depth knowledge of software applications, including architecture, design patterns, and scalability considerations
* Strong understanding of load testing tools, such as Apache JMeter, Gatling, or Locust
* Experience with cloud-based infrastructure, including AWS or Azure
* Familiarity with containerization technologies, such as Docker
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with ability to interpret complex data sets
* Strong communication and collaboration skills, with experience working with cross-functional teams
