**Personal Care Aides (overlap)**

**Responsibilities**

* Provide personal care services, including bathing, dressing, and grooming
* Assist with daily living activities, such as meal preparation and medication management

* High school diploma or equivalent required; CNA certification preferred

* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
* Compassionate and empathetic approach to care delivery
