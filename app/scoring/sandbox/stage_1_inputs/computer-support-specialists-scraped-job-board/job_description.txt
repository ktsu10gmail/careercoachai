* Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues via phone, email, or in-person

* Develop and maintain knowledge of new technologies and systems
* Provide exceptional customer service and support
* Document all interactions and resolutions using our ticketing system

**Required Skills:**

* 1-2 years of experience in computer support or a related field
* Strong technical skills, including Windows, macOS, and Linux operating systems
* Experience with hardware troubleshooting and repair (optional)

* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
