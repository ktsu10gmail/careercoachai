**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to design, develop, and execute comprehensive stress testing scenarios for our scoring engine. The successful candidate will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize test cases, develop test plans, and ensure the reliability and performance of our scoring engine under various loads.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop stress testing scenarios to simulate real-world usage patterns and identify potential bottlenecks in the scoring engine
* Collaborate with development teams to gather requirements and ensure test cases align with project goals
* Develop and execute comprehensive test plans, including data generation, test execution, and results analysis
* Identify and report defects or issues found during testing, working closely with development teams to reproduce and resolve them
* Analyze test results and provide recommendations for process improvements and optimization of the scoring engine
* Maintain accurate records of test cases, test plans, and results, ensuring data integrity and compliance with regulatory requirements
* Participate in continuous improvement initiatives, contributing to the development of new testing methodologies and tools

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field; Master's degree preferred
* 3+ years of experience in stress testing, performance testing, or a related field
* Strong understanding of software engineering principles, including design patterns, algorithms, and data structures
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with test automation frameworks (e.g., Selenium, Appium) and scripting languages (e.g., Python, Ruby)
* Knowledge of database management systems and data modeling techniques
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with ability to identify and prioritize testing efforts

**Preferred Skills:**

* Experience with cloud-based infrastructure and containerization technologies (e.g., AWS, Docker)
* Familiarity with machine learning or artificial intelligence frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch)
* Knowledge of DevOps practices and tools (e.g., Jenkins, GitLab CI/CD)
* Certification in stress testing or performance testing methodologies (e.g., ISTQB, CSTE)
