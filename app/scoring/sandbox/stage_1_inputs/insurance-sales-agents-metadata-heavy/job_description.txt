**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine

**Job Attributes:**

* Salary Range: $120,000 - $180,000 per year
* Work Mode: Full-time, on-site
* Location: San Francisco, CA (with occasional remote work)
* Employment Type: Permanent
* Target Start Date: 6 weeks from hire date
* Travel: Occasional travel to industry conferences and training events (up to 10% of the time)

* Security Clearance: Not required, but a valid passport is necessary for occasional remote work

**Job Description:**

We are seeking an experienced Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine to join our team. As a key member of our Quality Assurance team, you will be responsible for designing and executing comprehensive stress testing scenarios to ensure the reliability and performance of our scoring engine.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute stress testing scenarios to identify potential bottlenecks and areas for improvement in the scoring engine
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, product management, and operations, to understand system requirements and develop test plans
* Develop and maintain a comprehensive understanding of the scoring engine's architecture, data flows, and performance characteristics
* Execute stress testing scenarios using various tools and techniques, including load testing, performance testing, and endurance testing
* Analyze test results, identify trends and patterns, and provide actionable recommendations for improvement
* Work closely with the development team to reproduce issues found during testing and collaborate on resolving them
* Develop and maintain a library of reusable test scripts and frameworks to improve efficiency and reduce testing time

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering, preferably in a metadata-heavy environment
* Strong understanding of software architecture, data structures, and algorithms
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with load testing tools, such as Apache JMeter or Gatling
* Knowledge of performance testing frameworks, such as Selenium or Cypress
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to identify complex issues and develop creative solutions
* Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams
* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field
