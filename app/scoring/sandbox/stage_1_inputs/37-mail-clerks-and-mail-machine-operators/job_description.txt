Responsibilities:

- Sorts and processes incoming and outgoing mail, packages, and parcels according to established procedures
- Operates postal equipment such as mail sorters, scanners, and postage meters
- Reads and interprets mail labels, addresses, and barcodes to ensure accurate sorting and delivery
- Maintains accurate records of mail processing, including tracking numbers and delivery confirmations
- Assists with mail-related tasks, such as addressing envelopes, affixing postage, and preparing packages for shipping
- Collaborates with other postal workers to achieve daily mail processing goals and objectives

Required Skills:

- Basic knowledge of postal procedures and regulations
- Ability to operate postal equipment, including manual sorters and scanners
- Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
- Excellent communication and customer service skills
- Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and stand for long periods

Preferred Skills:

- Experience with automated mail processing systems
- Familiarity with barcode scanning technology
- Knowledge of postal codes and zip code mapping
- Certification in postal operations or a related field
