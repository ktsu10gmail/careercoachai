**Tailors and Dressmakers**

We are seeking skilled professionals to join our team as Tailors and Dressmakers. As a key member of our design and production team, you will be responsible for creating high-quality garments that meet our customers' needs.

**Job Summary:**
As a Tailor or Dressmaker, you will work closely with our design team to bring our designs to life. You will be responsible for measuring and fitting clients, creating prototypes, and producing final garments using your expertise in sewing, pattern-making, and textile manipulation.

**Responsibilities:**

* Measure and fit clients to create custom garments
* Create prototypes and make adjustments as needed
* Produce high-quality garments using a variety of techniques and materials
* Work with the design team to bring designs to life
* Maintain a clean and organized workspace
* Meet production deadlines and manage workload effectively
* Provide excellent customer service and communicate effectively with clients

**Required Skills:**

Java, Python, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign), Microsoft Office, Sketchbook Pro, Tailor's Ham, Sewing Machine, Serger, Overlock Machine, Cutting Table, Measuring Tape, Pattern-Making Software (e.g. Adobe Illustrator), Textile Finishing Techniques, Garment Construction Methods, Quality Control Procedures, Time Management and Organization, Attention to Detail, Communication Skills.

**Preferred Qualifications:**

* 2+ years of experience in tailoring or dressmaking
* Knowledge of garment construction methods and textile manipulation techniques
* Experience with pattern-making software and computer-aided design (CAD) programs
* Familiarity with industry-standard tools and equipment
* Strong attention to detail and ability to work accurately under pressure

**What We Offer:**

* Competitive salary and benefits package
* Opportunities for professional growth and development
* Collaborative and dynamic work environment
* Access to the latest technology and equipment
* Recognition and rewards for outstanding performance
