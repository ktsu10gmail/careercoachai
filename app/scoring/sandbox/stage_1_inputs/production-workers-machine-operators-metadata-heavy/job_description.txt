**Job Description:**

We are seeking highly motivated and detail-oriented Production Workers / Machine Operators to join our team. As a key member of our production team, you will be responsible for operating complex machinery and equipment to produce high-quality products.

**Responsibilities:**

* Operate and maintain various types of machinery and equipment, including scoring engines, to meet production targets
* Monitor and control machine performance in real-time to ensure optimal output and quality
* Perform routine maintenance tasks, such as cleaning and lubricating equipment, to prevent downtime and optimize productivity
* Collaborate with other team members to resolve production issues and improve overall efficiency
* Maintain accurate records of production data, including metrics on machine performance and product quality
* Participate in training and development programs to stay up-to-date on new technologies and techniques

**Required Skills:**

* 1-2 years of experience operating complex machinery and equipment in a manufacturing environment
* Strong understanding of scoring engine operation and maintenance
* Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, dynamic environment with attention to detail
* Excellent communication and teamwork skills
* High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree or higher preferred
* Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and stand for long periods of time
* Familiarity with lean manufacturing principles and quality control procedures
