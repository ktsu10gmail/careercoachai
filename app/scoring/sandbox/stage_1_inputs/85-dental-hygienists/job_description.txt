Responsibilities:

* Conduct thorough dental examinations, including visual inspections of teeth and gums, to identify signs of oral diseases
* Provide personalized oral hygiene instructions and demonstrations to patients, including proper brushing and flossing techniques
* Administer local anesthetics and perform periodontal treatments as needed
* Take and develop radiographs (x-rays) of patients' teeth and gums
* Develop and implement treatment plans for patients with dental caries, gum disease, and other oral health issues
* Educate patients on the importance of regular dental check-ups and preventative care
* Maintain accurate and up-to-date patient records, including medical and dental histories

Required Skills:

* Associate's degree in dental hygiene from an accredited program
* Current licensure as a registered dental hygienist (RDH) in the state where you practice
* Basic life support certification
* Ability to communicate effectively with patients of all ages and backgrounds
* Strong organizational and time management skills, with ability to prioritize tasks and manage multiple patients simultaneously

Preferred Skills:

* Experience working in a clinical setting or private practice
* Knowledge of dental materials and equipment, including ultrasonic scalers and air polisher units
* Familiarity with electronic medical records (EMRs) and dental software systems
* Certification in pediatric dentistry or orthodontic hygiene
* Bilingual or multilingual skills
