**Business Development Representative**

We are seeking highly motivated and results-driven Business Development Representatives to join our team. As a Business Development Representative, you will be responsible for identifying new business opportunities, building relationships with key decision-makers, and driving revenue growth.

**Responsibilities:**

* Conduct thorough research on potential clients and identify high-potential leads
* Develop and execute personalized sales strategies to engage with target accounts
* Build strong relationships with key decision-makers through regular communication and follow-up
* Present product offerings and demonstrate value proposition to prospects
* Meet or exceed monthly sales targets and contribute to overall revenue growth
* Collaborate with internal teams, including marketing and customer success, to ensure seamless customer experiences
* Continuously monitor market trends and competitor activity to stay ahead of the competition

**Required Skills:**

* 2+ years of experience in business development, sales, or a related field
* Proven track record of meeting or exceeding sales targets
* Excellent communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills
* Strong understanding of product offerings and value proposition
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
* Proficiency in CRM software and Microsoft Office applications

**Preferred Skills:**

* Experience working in the technology or finance industries
* Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, or a related field
* Familiarity with sales enablement tools and platforms
* Certification in sales or business development (e.g. Certified Sales Professional)
* Fluency in multiple languages
