**Job Title:** Quality Assurance Testers - Domain Scope Trap Stress Testing

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking experienced Quality Assurance Testers to perform stress testing on our scoring engine, specifically focusing on domain scope trap scenarios. The successful candidate will be responsible for simulating high-volume traffic and identifying performance bottlenecks to ensure the reliability and scalability of our system.

**Responsibilities:**

* Develop and execute comprehensive test plans to simulate various domain scope trap scenarios, including but not limited to:
 + High-traffic simulations with multiple concurrent users
 + Large-scale data imports and exports
 + System crashes and recovery tests
 + Edge cases and boundary testing
* Utilize specialized tools and software to generate realistic traffic patterns and stress the scoring engine
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering and product management, to identify and prioritize test cases
* Develop and maintain detailed test reports, including results, analysis, and recommendations for improvement
* Participate in regular testing cycles and provide timely feedback on system performance

**Required Skills:**

* 3+ years of experience in quality assurance testing or a related field
* In-depth knowledge of stress testing methodologies and tools (e.g. Apache JMeter, Gatling)
* Strong understanding of software development life cycles and testing frameworks
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with ability to identify performance bottlenecks
* Experience working with regulated industries (e.g. finance, healthcare) is a plus
* Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities and deadlines

**Domain Qualifiers:**

* Software/SaaS
* Enterprise
* Regulated industry (essential)
* Global
* Multi-site
* High-volume
* Low-latency

**Scope Qualifiers:**

* Domain scope trap scenarios only
* Stress testing of scoring engine
* No other system components or features will be tested
