**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer - Domain Scope Trap

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to design, develop, and execute comprehensive stress testing scenarios for our billing and posting systems. The successful candidate will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize testing efforts, ensuring the reliability and performance of our systems under various loads.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop stress testing scenarios to simulate high-volume transactions, low-latency requirements, and multi-site interactions
* Collaborate with product management, engineering, and quality assurance teams to understand system requirements and identify testing gaps
* Develop and execute test plans, scripts, and tools to simulate various user behaviors and system loads
* Analyze test results, identify trends, and provide recommendations for system improvements
* Work closely with the development team to reproduce and resolve issues identified during testing
* Maintain accurate records of testing efforts, including test plans, results, and recommendations

**Required Skills:**

* 3+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering
* Strong understanding of billing and posting systems, including domain scope trap functionality
* Proficiency in scripting languages such as Python, Java, or C#
* Experience with load testing tools, such as Apache JMeter or Gatling
* Knowledge of cloud-based infrastructure and scalability principles
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
* Ability to work independently and collaboratively as part of a team

**Domain Qualifiers:**

* Software/SaaS: Enterprise billing and posting systems
* Scope Qualifiers:
	+ Regulated industry: Financial services, healthcare, or government
	+ Global: Testing must be performed on multiple continents with varying time zones
	+ Multi-site: Testing must simulate interactions between multiple sites or locations
	+ High-volume: Testing must simulate high transaction volumes to ensure system performance under load
	+ Low-latency: Testing must prioritize low latency requirements for real-time transactions

**Note:** This job posting is subject to change, and only candidates who meet the essential qualifications will be considered.
