**Job Description:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine to join our team. As a key member of our engineering team, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and executing comprehensive stress testing scenarios for our scoring engine.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop realistic stress test scenarios that simulate real-world usage patterns
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product management, QA, and sales to understand business requirements and identify areas for improvement
* Develop and maintain a thorough understanding of the scoring engine's architecture, components, and performance characteristics
* Execute stress tests using various tools and techniques, including load testing, performance benchmarking, and vulnerability scanning
* Analyze test results, identify trends and patterns, and provide actionable recommendations to improve the scoring engine's reliability and performance
* Develop and maintain documentation of test plans, test cases, and test results
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in stress testing and performance engineering

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience in stress testing, performance engineering, or a related field
* In-depth knowledge of scoring engine architecture, components, and performance characteristics
* Strong understanding of load testing tools, such as Apache JMeter, Gatling, or Locust
* Experience with performance benchmarking tools, such as Apache Bench or NeoLoad
* Familiarity with vulnerability scanning tools, such as OWASP ZAP or Burp Suite
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to identify trends and patterns in complex data sets
* Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams
* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
