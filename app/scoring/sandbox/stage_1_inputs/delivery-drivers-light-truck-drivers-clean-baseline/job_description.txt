Job Title: Delivery Drivers / Light Truck Drivers

**Job Summary**

We are seeking reliable and safety-conscious individuals to join our team as Delivery Drivers/ Light Truck Drivers. As a key member of our logistics team, you will be responsible for safely transporting packages and goods to customers in a timely manner.

**Responsibilities**

* Safely operate a company-provided light truck or van to transport packages and goods
* Follow all traffic laws and regulations, including hours of service and weight limits
* Provide excellent customer service by delivering packages on time and in good condition
* Maintain a clean and organized vehicle, both inside and out
* Scan packages and update delivery records accurately and efficiently
* Work collaboratively with dispatch team to ensure timely delivery of packages
* Perform pre- and post-trip inspections of vehicle to ensure safety and efficiency
* Adhere to company policies and procedures for handling hazardous materials and other special cargo

**Required Skills**

* Valid driver's license with a clean driving record
* Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and stand for long periods
* High school diploma or equivalent required; some college preferred
* Excellent communication and customer service skills
* Basic math skills and ability to handle cash transactions
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team

**Preferred Skills**

* Previous experience as a delivery driver or in a related field
* Knowledge of local traffic laws and regulations
* Familiarity with GPS navigation systems and mobile apps
* Ability to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends
* Basic mechanical skills and ability to perform basic vehicle maintenance tasks
