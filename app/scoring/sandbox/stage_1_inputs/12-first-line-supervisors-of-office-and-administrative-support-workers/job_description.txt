Responsibilities:

* Provide guidance, support, and feedback to office and administrative support workers to ensure they meet performance standards
* Conduct regular one-on-one meetings with team members to discuss progress, address concerns, and set goals
* Develop and implement training programs to enhance skills and knowledge of support staff
* Manage workload and prioritize tasks to ensure efficient use of resources
* Coordinate with other departments as needed to resolve issues or complete projects
* Monitor and track key performance indicators (KPIs) such as productivity, accuracy, and customer satisfaction
* Address employee complaints and concerns in a fair and timely manner

Required Skills:

* High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree preferred
* 2+ years of supervisory experience in an office or administrative setting
* Proven ability to lead and manage teams effectively
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to analyze data and make informed decisions
* Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite, particularly Excel, Word, and Outlook

Preferred Skills:

* Bachelor's degree in business administration, human resources, or related field
* Experience with performance management software such as Workday or ADP
* Familiarity with HR policies and procedures
* Certification in supervisor training programs such as SHRM-CP or PHR
* Bilingual or multilingual skills
