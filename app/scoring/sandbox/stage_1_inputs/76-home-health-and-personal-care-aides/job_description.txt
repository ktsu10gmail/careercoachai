Responsibilities:

* Provide personal care and assistance to clients with physical disabilities, chronic illnesses, or cognitive impairments in their homes
* Assist clients with daily living activities such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting
* Administer medications as prescribed by a healthcare provider
* Prepare and serve nutritious meals and snacks according to client preferences and dietary needs
* Maintain a safe and clean environment for clients, including laundry, cleaning, and organization of living spaces
* Develop and maintain positive relationships with clients, families, and healthcare providers through effective communication and empathy
* Document client interactions, progress, and concerns in a timely and accurate manner

Required Skills:

* High school diploma or equivalent required; CNA certification preferred
* Ability to lift 50 pounds and stand for long periods of time
* Basic life support skills, including CPR and first aid training
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
* Familiarity with medical equipment and devices, such as oxygen tanks and walkers

Preferred Skills:

* Experience working in home health or personal care settings
* Knowledge of nutrition and meal planning principles
* Certification in first aid and CPR renewal every 2 years
* Ability to speak multiple languages (Spanish, Chinese, etc.)
* Familiarity with electronic medical records (EMRs)
