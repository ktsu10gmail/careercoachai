**Job Title: Budget Analysts**

We're seeking highly skilled and motivated Budget Analysts to join our team. As a key member of our finance department, you'll play a critical role in driving business growth through data-driven insights and expert analysis.

**Responsibilities**

* Develop and maintain accurate financial models and forecasts
* Analyze budget variance reports and provide recommendations for improvement
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify areas for cost savings and process optimization
* Prepare and present financial reports to senior management and stakeholders
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and regulatory changes, applying this knowledge to inform business decisions

**Required Skills**

* Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in budget analysis and financial planning
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
* Excellent communication and presentation skills
* Proficiency in Microsoft Office and financial modeling software (e.g., Excel, Access)
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
