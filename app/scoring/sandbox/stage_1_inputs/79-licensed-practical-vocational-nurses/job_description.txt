Responsibilities:

* Provide direct patient care under the supervision of a registered nurse, including administering medications, performing vital signs, and providing wound care
* Develop and implement individualized care plans for patients with complex medical needs, such as diabetes, hypertension, or chronic pain management
* Conduct routine assessments to identify changes in patient condition and report findings to the registered nurse
* Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to coordinate patient care and develop discharge plans
* Educate patients and families on disease management, self-care techniques, and medication adherence
* Maintain accurate and up-to-date patient records, including progress notes and medication administration records

Required Skills:

* Current licensure as a Licensed Practical/Vocational Nurse (LPN) or Licensed Vocational Nurse (LVN)
* Valid CPR certification
* Basic life support skills
* Ability to lift 50 pounds and stand for long periods
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

Preferred Skills:

* Experience working with patients with complex medical needs, such as those requiring dialysis or ventilator care
* Knowledge of electronic medical records (EMRs) and ability to navigate clinical software systems
* Certification in wound care or other specialized areas of practice
* Bilingual or multilingual skills
* Ability to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends
