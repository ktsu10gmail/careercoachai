**About Us:**
At Acme Inc., we're dedicated to delivering exceptional results and unparalleled customer experiences. We're seeking a highly skilled and organized Executive Assistant to join our team.

**Job Summary:**

We're looking for an experienced and detail-oriented Executive Assistant to provide administrative support to our senior leadership team. As the first point of contact, you'll be responsible for managing calendars, coordinating travel arrangements, preparing correspondence, and ensuring seamless day-to-day operations.

**Responsibilities:**

* Manage the schedules of multiple executives, including scheduling appointments, meetings, and events
* Coordinate travel arrangements, including booking flights, hotels, and rental cars
* Prepare and edit correspondence, reports, and presentations for senior leadership
* Develop and maintain complex spreadsheets and databases to track project progress and performance metrics
* Provide exceptional customer service to internal stakeholders and external clients
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to achieve business objectives

**Required Skills:**

* 3+ years of experience as an Executive Assistant or in a similar role
* Proven track record of providing exceptional administrative support
* Excellent organizational, communication, and problem-solving skills
* Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook)
* Experience with calendar management tools (e.g., Google Calendar, Microsoft Exchange)

* 401(k) matching program
