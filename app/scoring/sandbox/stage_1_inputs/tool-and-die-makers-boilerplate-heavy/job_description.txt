**Job Title: Tool and Die Makers**

We're seeking skilled Tool and Die Makers to join our team! As a Tool and Die Maker, you'll play a critical role in designing, building, and maintaining the tools and dies that power our manufacturing operations. If you're passionate about precision engineering and have a keen eye for detail, we want to hear from you!

**Responsibilities**

* Design, build, and maintain complex tooling systems
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop new products and processes
* Conduct quality control inspections and troubleshoot issues
* Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and productivity
* Maintain accurate records of production data and inventory management

**Required Skills**

* 3+ years of experience in tool and die making or a related field
* Bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering, manufacturing engineering, or a related field
* Strong understanding of precision machining techniques and materials
* Proficiency in CAD/CAM software (e.g. SolidWorks, Autodesk Inventor)
* Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
* Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities
