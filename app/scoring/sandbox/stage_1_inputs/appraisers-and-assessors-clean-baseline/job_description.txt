**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team of experts in evaluating the performance and reliability of complex systems. As a Stress Test Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and executing stress tests on our scoring engine to ensure its stability, scalability, and security.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute comprehensive stress tests on the scoring engine to identify potential bottlenecks and areas for improvement
* Develop and maintain test plans, scripts, and tools to simulate various types of loads and workloads
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including development, operations, and quality assurance, to ensure alignment and effective testing
* Analyze test results, identify trends and patterns, and provide actionable recommendations for optimization and improvement
* Develop and maintain documentation of test procedures, results, and findings
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in stress testing and performance evaluation

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in stress testing, performance evaluation, or a related field
* Strong understanding of software development life cycles, testing methodologies, and quality assurance principles
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with test automation frameworks and tools (e.g., Selenium, JUnit)
* Knowledge of cloud-based infrastructure and scalability concepts

**Preferred Skills:**

* Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 5+ years of experience in stress testing, performance evaluation, or a related field
* Experience with containerization technologies (e.g., Docker) and orchestration tools (e.g., Kubernetes)
* Familiarity with machine learning and artificial intelligence concepts
* Certification in stress testing or performance evaluation methodologies (e.g., ISTQB, CSTE)

**What We Offer:**

* Competitive salary and benefits package
* Opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects and technologies
* Collaborative and dynamic work environment
* Professional development and growth opportunities
