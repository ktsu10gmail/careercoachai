**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team of engineers responsible for designing, developing, and testing complex systems. As a Stress Test Engineer, you will be responsible for creating realistic stress tests to identify performance bottlenecks in our scoring engine.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop comprehensive stress test scenarios to simulate real-world usage patterns
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and ensure test coverage
* Develop and execute automated testing scripts using various programming languages and tools
* Analyze test results, identify performance bottlenecks, and provide recommendations for optimization
* Work closely with the development team to implement changes and verify fixes
* Maintain accurate records of test results, including data analysis and reporting

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in stress testing, performance engineering, or a related field
* Strong understanding of software development life cycles and testing methodologies
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with automated testing frameworks and tools (e.g. JMeter, Gatling)
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills

**Preferred Skills:**

* Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* Experience with cloud-based systems and scalability testing
* Knowledge of containerization technologies (e.g. Docker) and orchestration tools (e.g. Kubernetes)
* Familiarity with machine learning and artificial intelligence concepts
* Certification in stress testing or performance engineering (e.g. ISTQB)
