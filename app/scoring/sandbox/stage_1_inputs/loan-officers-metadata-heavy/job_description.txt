**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine

**Job Attributes:**

* Salary Range: $120,000 - $180,000 per year
* Work Mode: Full-time, on-site
* Location: San Francisco, CA (with occasional travel to New York and London)
* Employment Type: Permanent
* Target Start Date: 6 weeks from hire date
* Travel: Up to 20% of the work time will be spent at client sites in the US and Europe
* Sponsorship: The company will sponsor a security clearance for the selected candidate
* Security Clearance: Top Secret (required)

**Job Description:**

We are seeking an experienced Stress Test Engineer to join our team, responsible for designing, developing, and executing comprehensive stress testing scenarios for our scoring engine. As a key member of our Quality Assurance team, you will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize test cases, develop test scripts, and execute tests to ensure the reliability and performance of our scoring engine.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop comprehensive stress testing scenarios for the scoring engine, including load testing, endurance testing, and performance testing
* Collaborate with product management, engineering, and QA teams to identify and prioritize test cases
* Develop and maintain test scripts, data sets, and other supporting materials for stress tests
* Execute stress tests on the scoring engine, monitoring results and identifying issues or areas for improvement
* Analyze test results, provide feedback to the development team, and work with them to implement fixes and improvements
* Develop and maintain documentation of testing procedures, test scripts, and results
* Participate in continuous integration and delivery processes, ensuring that tests are executed as part of the build process

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering for software applications
* Strong understanding of scoring engine architecture and functionality
* Experience with load testing tools such as Apache JMeter, Gatling, or Locust
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++
* Experience with data analysis and visualization tools such as Excel, Tableau, or Power BI
* Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams
* Strong problem-solving skills, with ability to analyze complex issues and develop creative solutions
