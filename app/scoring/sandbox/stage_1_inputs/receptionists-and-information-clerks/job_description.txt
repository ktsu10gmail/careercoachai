Responsibilities:

* Manage front desk operations, including answering phone calls, responding to emails, and greeting visitors
* Process incoming mail, packages, and documents, and distribute them as necessary
* Provide information and answer questions about company policies, procedures, and services
* Maintain accurate records and files, both physical and digital
* Perform data entry tasks using software such as Microsoft Office or Google Suite
* Assist with event planning, registration, and coordination
* Handle cash transactions and operate a point-of-sale system
* Develop and maintain relationships with clients, vendors, and colleagues

Required Skills:

* High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree preferred
* 1-2 years of experience in a receptionist or administrative role
* Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook)
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
* Basic knowledge of data entry software and point-of-sale systems

Preferred Skills:

* Experience with Google Suite or other cloud-based productivity tools
* Familiarity with customer relationship management (CRM) software
* Knowledge of office management principles and procedures
* Certification in medical terminology or other specialized field
* Bilingualism or multilingualism in a second language
