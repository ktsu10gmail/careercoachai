Responsibilities:

- Process payroll for a diverse workforce of 500 employees, ensuring accurate and timely payment of wages and benefits.
- Maintain accurate records of employee hours worked, including tracking overtime and leave time.
- Prepare and distribute pay stubs to employees via email or mail.
- Reconcile payroll discrepancies and investigate errors in a timely manner.
- Assist with the implementation of new payroll systems and processes as required by management.
- Provide support for special projects, such as processing bonuses or adjusting employee classifications.

Required Skills:

- High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree preferred.
- 1-2 years of experience in payroll or timekeeping operations.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, particularly Excel and Word.
- Familiarity with payroll software, such as ADP Workforce Now or Paychex.
- Strong organizational and attention-to-detail skills.

Preferred Skills:

- Experience with HRIS systems, such as Workday or BambooHR.
- Knowledge of federal and state labor laws, including FLSA and FMLA.
- Certification in payroll processing, such as CPP or SPHR.
- Bilingual or multilingual skills, particularly in languages commonly spoken by the workforce.
