**Job Title: Construction Laborers**

We're seeking skilled and motivated Construction Laborers to join our team! As a Construction Laborer, you'll play a critical role in the success of our projects, working closely with our experienced construction teams to deliver high-quality results on time and on budget.

**Responsibilities**

* Assist in the construction process by performing manual labor tasks such as excavation, demolition, and material handling
* Operate heavy equipment and tools under the supervision of a qualified supervisor or foreman
* Maintain a safe working environment by adhering to company safety policies and procedures
* Collaborate with other team members to complete projects efficiently and effectively
* Perform various administrative tasks as required

**Required Skills**

* High school diploma or equivalent required; vocational training in construction or related field preferred
* 1-2 years of experience in construction laboring or a related field
* Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced environment
* Valid driver's license and clean driving record
* Excellent communication and teamwork skills
