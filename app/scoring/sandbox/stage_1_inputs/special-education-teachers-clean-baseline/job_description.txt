**Job Title:** Scoring Engine Stress Test Engineer

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Scoring Engine Stress Test Engineer to join our team. As a key member of our engineering team, you will be responsible for designing and executing stress tests on our scoring engine to ensure its reliability, performance, and scalability.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute comprehensive stress tests on the scoring engine to identify performance bottlenecks and areas for improvement
* Develop and maintain test plans, scripts, and tools to simulate various workloads and scenarios
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product management, engineering, and operations, to understand business requirements and ensure test coverage
* Analyze test results, identify trends, and provide recommendations for optimization and improvement
* Work closely with the development team to reproduce issues and collaborate on fixes
* Develop and maintain documentation of testing procedures, test plans, and results

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in software testing, engineering, or a related field
* Strong understanding of computer systems, algorithms, and data structures
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++
* Experience with stress testing tools and frameworks (e.g. Apache JMeter, Gatling)
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
* Strong communication and collaboration skills

**Preferred Skills:**

* Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 5+ years of experience in software testing, engineering, or a related field
* Experience with cloud-based technologies (e.g. AWS, Azure)
* Knowledge of containerization technologies (e.g. Docker)
* Certification in stress testing or performance engineering
