**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team of experts in evaluating the performance and reliability of complex systems. As a Senior Stress Test Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and executing comprehensive stress testing scenarios to identify potential weaknesses and vulnerabilities in our scoring engine.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop realistic stress test scenarios to simulate various types of loads, including high traffic, concurrent requests, and resource-intensive operations
* Execute stress tests on the scoring engine, monitoring performance metrics and identifying areas for improvement
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, product management, and quality assurance, to ensure alignment and effective communication
* Develop and maintain test plans, scripts, and tools to support stress testing efforts
* Analyze results, identify trends, and provide recommendations for optimizing system performance and reliability
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in stress testing and performance engineering

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field; Master's degree preferred
* 5+ years of experience in stress testing, performance engineering, or a related field
* Strong understanding of software development life cycles, including design, implementation, and deployment
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++
* Experience with test automation frameworks and tools, such as JUnit, PyUnit, or Selenium
* Knowledge of cloud-based infrastructure and containerization technologies, such as AWS or Docker

**Preferred Skills:**

* Experience with scoring engines, machine learning, or artificial intelligence
* Familiarity with Agile development methodologies and version control systems, such as Git
* Certification in stress testing or performance engineering, such as ISTQB or CSTE
* Experience with data analysis tools, such as Excel, Tableau, or Power BI
