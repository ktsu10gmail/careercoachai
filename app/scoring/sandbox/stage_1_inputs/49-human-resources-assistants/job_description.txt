Responsibilities:

- Process employee onboarding documents, ensuring compliance with company policies and procedures
- Maintain accurate and up-to-date employee records in HRIS systems, including personnel files and benefits information
- Coordinate new hire orientations, training sessions, and other HR-related events
- Assist in the development and implementation of employee recognition programs
- Provide support for employee relations issues, including mediating conflicts and facilitating resolutions
- Conduct routine background checks on new hires and ensure compliance with company policies
- Prepare and distribute employee communications, such as company-wide announcements and policy updates

Required Skills:

- High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree in human resources or related field preferred
- 1-2 years of experience in HR or a related field
- Proficiency in HRIS systems, including ADP Workforce Now or similar software
- Strong organizational and communication skills
- Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information

Preferred Skills:

- Experience with employee engagement platforms, such as Culture Amp or 15Five
- Knowledge of employment laws and regulations, including Title VII and FLSA
- Certification in HR or a related field, such as SHRM-CP or HRCI certification
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint
