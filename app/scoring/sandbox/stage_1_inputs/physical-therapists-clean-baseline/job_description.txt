**Physical Therapist - Stress Test**

We are seeking a highly skilled Physical Therapist to join our team as a Scoring Engine Stress Tester. As a key member of our research and development team, you will play a critical role in evaluating the performance of our scoring engine under various stress conditions.

**Responsibilities:**

* Conduct thorough testing of the scoring engine using standardized test protocols
* Identify and report defects or issues with the scoring engine's performance
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to reproduce and troubleshoot defects
* Develop and maintain detailed test plans and execution reports
* Provide input on design improvements and feature enhancements
* Participate in code reviews and contribute to the development of new features

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Physical Therapy or related field
* 2+ years of experience in stress testing or performance evaluation
* Strong understanding of scoring engine architecture and functionality
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
* Ability to work independently and collaboratively as part of a team
* Proficiency in test automation tools (e.g. Selenium, Appium)

**Preferred Skills:**

* Master's degree in Physical Therapy or related field
* Experience with cloud-based scoring engines or similar technologies
* Familiarity with Agile development methodologies
* Knowledge of machine learning algorithms and their application to scoring engine performance
