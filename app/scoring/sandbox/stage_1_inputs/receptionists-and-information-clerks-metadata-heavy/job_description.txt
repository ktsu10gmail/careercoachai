**Job Description:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine to join our team. As a key member of our engineering team, you will be responsible for designing and executing comprehensive stress tests on our scoring engine to ensure its reliability, scalability, and performance.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute stress tests on the scoring engine to identify bottlenecks and areas for improvement
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product management, engineering, and operations, to understand requirements and develop test plans
* Develop and maintain a comprehensive testing framework to ensure thorough coverage of the scoring engine's functionality
* Analyze test results, identify issues, and work with the development team to resolve defects and implement fixes
* Develop and maintain technical documentation, including test scripts, reports, and dashboards
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to continuously improve testing methodologies

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering
* In-depth knowledge of scoring engine architecture and functionality
* Strong understanding of software development life cycles, including Agile and DevOps practices
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with test automation frameworks, including Pytest, Unittest, or JUnit
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to identify complex issues and develop effective solutions
* Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams
