Responsibilities:

* Conduct on-site inspections of properties to determine their value
* Review and analyze market data, sales records, and other relevant information to estimate property values
* Prepare written appraisals and reports that provide a detailed explanation of the valuation methodology used
* Testify in court as an expert witness if necessary
* Maintain accurate and up-to-date records of all appraisal work performed
* Stay current with changes in local, state, and federal laws and regulations related to property taxation

Required Skills:

* Bachelor's degree in a field such as real estate, business, or finance
* Completion of a state-approved appraiser training program
* 2-5 years of experience as an appraiser or assessor
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Preferred Skills:

* Proficiency in software such as AutoCAD, Excel, or GIS mapping tools
* Experience with property tax assessment and appeals processes
* Knowledge of local market trends and conditions
* Certification as a Certified General Appraiser (CGA) or other professional designations
* Bilingual or multilingual skills
