**Social Media Manager/Specialist**

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Social Media Manager/Specialist to join our team. As a key member of our marketing department, you will be responsible for developing and implementing social media strategies that drive engagement, grow our online presence, and support our brand's overall marketing goals.

**Responsibilities:**

* Develop and execute comprehensive social media strategies across multiple platforms (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube)
* Create and curate high-quality, engaging content (text, images, videos) that resonates with our target audience
* Manage and schedule posts using social media management tools (e.g. Hootsuite, Sprout Social)
* Monitor and respond to comments, messages, and reviews in a timely and professional manner
* Analyze social media metrics and adjust strategies accordingly to optimize performance
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams (marketing, communications, customer service) to ensure cohesive brand messaging and consistent online presence
* Stay up-to-date on the latest social media trends, best practices, and platform updates

**Required Skills:**

* 3+ years of experience in social media marketing or a related field
* Proven track record of success in developing and executing social media strategies that drive engagement and growth
* Strong understanding of social media platforms, including Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, and YouTube
* Excellent content creation and curation skills, with the ability to create high-quality content across multiple formats (text, images, videos)
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to interpret social media metrics and adjust strategies accordingly
* Experience with social media management tools (e.g. Hootsuite, Sprout Social) is a plus

**Preferred Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or a related field
* Experience working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment
* Knowledge of SEO principles and how to optimize content for search engines
* Familiarity with social media advertising platforms (e.g. Facebook Ads, Twitter Ads)
* Certification in social media marketing (e.g. Hootsuite Social Media Marketing Certification) is a plus
