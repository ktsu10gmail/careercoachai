**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team of engineers responsible for designing, developing, and testing high-performance systems. As a Stress Test Engineer, you will be responsible for creating realistic stress tests to identify performance bottlenecks in our scoring engine, ensuring it can handle large volumes of data and user traffic.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop comprehensive stress test scenarios to simulate real-world usage patterns
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and ensure test coverage
* Develop and execute automated testing scripts using various programming languages (e.g., Python, Java)
* Analyze test results, identify performance bottlenecks, and provide recommendations for optimization
* Work closely with the development team to implement changes and verify fixes
* Maintain accurate records of test results, including data analysis and visualization
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to continuously improve testing methodologies

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in stress testing, performance engineering, or a related field
* Strong understanding of computer systems, networking protocols, and software architecture
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, C++, or C#
* Experience with automated testing frameworks (e.g., Pytest, JUnit)
* Knowledge of cloud-based infrastructure and containerization technologies (e.g., AWS, Docker)

**Preferred Skills:**

* Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* Experience with machine learning, artificial intelligence, or data science
* Familiarity with scoring engine development and optimization techniques
* Certification in stress testing or performance engineering (e.g., ISTQB, PTE)
* Experience with Agile development methodologies and version control systems (e.g., Git)
