**Receptionists (Medical/Office)**

We're seeking highly organized and compassionate Receptionists to join our dynamic team!

**Responsibilities:**

* Manage front desk operations, including answering phone calls, responding to patient inquiries, and scheduling appointments
* Maintain accurate records and files, both physical and electronic
* Provide exceptional customer service to patients, families, and healthcare professionals
* Process payments and manage billing and insurance claims
* Perform administrative tasks as needed, such as data entry and mailings

**Required Skills:**

* High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree preferred
* 1-2 years of experience in a medical office setting or related field
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities
* Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, particularly Word, Excel, and Outlook
* Basic knowledge of medical terminology and coding systems (e.g. ICD-10)
