Job Title: Stress Test Engineer

Job Type: Full-time

Location: Remote (with occasional on-site visits)

Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team, responsible for designing and executing comprehensive stress testing scenarios to identify performance bottlenecks in our scoring engine. As a key member of our engineering team, you will work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure the reliability, scalability, and security of our platform.

Responsibilities:

* Design and execute stress testing scenarios to simulate real-world usage patterns and identify performance bottlenecks
* Collaborate with product management and engineering teams to understand system requirements and develop test plans
* Develop and maintain a comprehensive understanding of our scoring engine's architecture and functionality
* Analyze test results, identify trends, and provide actionable recommendations for improvement
* Work closely with QA teams to ensure test coverage and identify areas for optimization
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to inform testing strategies

Required Skills:

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 3+ years of experience in stress testing, performance engineering, or a related field
* Strong understanding of software architecture, scalability, and reliability principles
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with containerization tools like Docker and Kubernetes
* Familiarity with cloud-based infrastructure and deployment models

Preferred Skills:

* Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 5+ years of experience in stress testing, performance engineering, or a related field
* Experience with machine learning frameworks and algorithms
* Knowledge of containerization tools like Docker and Kubernetes
* Familiarity with cloud-based infrastructure and deployment models
