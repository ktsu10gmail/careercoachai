**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team. As a Stress Test Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and executing stress tests on our scoring engine to ensure its reliability, scalability, and performance under various loads and conditions.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop comprehensive stress test plans to identify and prioritize areas of improvement in the scoring engine
* Execute stress tests using a variety of tools and platforms, including but not limited to JMeter, Gatling, Locust, Apache JMeter, NeoLoad, LoadRunner, and Selenium
* Analyze test results and provide detailed reports on performance metrics, such as throughput, latency, and error rates
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including development, operations, and quality assurance, to identify and prioritize areas for improvement
* Develop and maintain a comprehensive understanding of the scoring engine's architecture, components, and interactions
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies in stress testing and performance engineering

**Required Skills:**

Java, Python, C++, JavaScript, JMeter, Gatling, Locust, Apache JMeter, NeoLoad, LoadRunner, Selenium, Docker, Kubernetes, Ansible, AWS CloudFormation, Azure DevOps, Git, Agile methodologies, Test-Driven Development (TDD), Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, Performance testing frameworks such as Apache Bench and Locust

**Nice to Have:**

* Experience with containerization using Docker
* Knowledge of cloud-based infrastructure and scalability models
* Familiarity with machine learning and artificial intelligence concepts
* Experience with DevOps tools such as Jenkins, Travis CI, or CircleCI
* Certification in stress testing or performance engineering (e.g. ISTQB, CSTE)

**What We Offer:**

* Competitive salary and benefits package
* Opportunity to work on a cutting-edge technology stack
* Collaborative and dynamic work environment
* Professional development opportunities and training
