Responsibilities:

* Conduct thorough testing of software applications to identify defects and ensure compliance with quality standards
* Develop and maintain test plans, test cases, and test scripts for assigned projects
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including development, design, and project management, to identify and report defects
* Create and manage defect reports, track defect status, and provide regular progress updates to stakeholders
* Participate in code reviews and provide feedback on testing methodologies and processes
* Develop and maintain test automation frameworks using tools such as Selenium, Appium, or TestComplete
* Conduct exploratory testing to identify defects that may not be caught by automated tests

Required Skills:

* 2+ years of experience in software testing, with a focus on quality assurance
* Strong understanding of software development life cycles and testing methodologies
* Proficiency in testing tools such as JIRA, TestRail, or Trello
* Experience with Agile development methodologies and version control systems (e.g. Git)
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to identify and report defects accurately

Preferred Skills:

* Experience with cloud-based testing frameworks and tools (e.g. AWS Device Farm, Google Cloud Test Lab)
* Knowledge of machine learning or artificial intelligence technologies
* Certification in ISTQB (International Software Testing Qualifications Board) or CSTE (Certified Software Test Engineer)
* Experience with continuous integration and delivery pipelines using tools such as Jenkins or GitLab CI/CD
