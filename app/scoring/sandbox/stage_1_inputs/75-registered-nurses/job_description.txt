**Registered Nurse**

**Responsibilities:**

* Provide high-quality patient care to assigned patients in a variety of settings, including acute care hospitals, clinics, and long-term care facilities
* Conduct thorough assessments of patients' physical and emotional needs, developing individualized care plans to address these needs
* Administer medications, treatments, and therapies as ordered by physicians and other healthcare professionals
* Monitor patients' vital signs, lab results, and other health indicators to identify potential problems early
* Collaborate with interdisciplinary teams, including doctors, therapists, and social workers, to coordinate patient care
* Educate patients and families on disease management, treatment options, and self-care techniques
* Document patient information accurately and thoroughly in electronic medical records

**Required Skills:**

* Current licensure as a Registered Nurse (RN) in the state of [insert state]
* Bachelor's degree in Nursing or related field
* 1-2 years of experience in acute care hospital setting
* Strong communication, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills
* Ability to work effectively in fast-paced environments with multiple priorities
* Basic life support certification (BLS) and advanced cardiovascular life support (ACLS) certification

**Preferred Skills:**

* Experience working with electronic medical records (EMRs)
* Familiarity with hospital information systems (HIS)
* Certification in a specialty area, such as pediatrics or oncology
* Bilingual or multilingual skills
* Experience with patient education and health promotion programs
