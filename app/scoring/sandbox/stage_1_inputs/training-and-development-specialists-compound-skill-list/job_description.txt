**Training and Development Specialist**

We are seeking a highly skilled Training and Development Specialist to join our team. As a key member of our Learning and Development department, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing training programs that meet the needs of our employees.

**Job Summary:**

The successful candidate will have a strong background in instructional design, adult learning theory, and technology-enhanced learning. They will work closely with subject matter experts to develop comprehensive training programs that align with business objectives. The ideal candidate will be able to analyze learning gaps, identify knowledge areas for improvement, and create engaging and effective training content.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design, develop, and implement training programs for employees at all levels of the organization
* Conduct needs assessments to identify learning gaps and opportunities for growth
* Collaborate with subject matter experts to develop comprehensive training content
* Create engaging and interactive training materials using a variety of media formats (e.g. video, audio, text)
* Evaluate the effectiveness of training programs and make recommendations for improvement
* Develop and maintain training records and reports
* Stay up-to-date on industry trends and best practices in training and development

**Required Skills:**

Instructional design principles and adult learning theory
Experience with e-learning authoring tools (e.g. Articulate Storyline, Adobe Captivate)
Familiarity with Learning Management Systems (LMS) such as Canvas, Blackboard, or Moodle
Knowledge of instructional technology platforms (e.g. Google Classroom, Microsoft Teams)
Experience with video production and editing software (e.g. Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro)
Strong communication and interpersonal skills
Ability to work independently and collaboratively as part of a team
Experience with data analysis and reporting tools (e.g. Excel, Tableau)
Familiarity with project management methodologies (e.g. Agile, Scrum)
Knowledge of accessibility guidelines for training content
Experience with gamification and simulation-based learning
Optional:

* Experience with virtual reality (VR) or augmented reality (AR) technology
* Familiarity with artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) tools
* Knowledge of cloud-based learning platforms (e.g. Google Cloud, Amazon Web Services)
* Experience with mobile learning development (e.g. iOS, Android)

**We Offer:**

Competitive salary and benefits package
Opportunity to work with a dynamic and growing organization
Professional development opportunities for growth and advancement
Collaborative and supportive team environment
