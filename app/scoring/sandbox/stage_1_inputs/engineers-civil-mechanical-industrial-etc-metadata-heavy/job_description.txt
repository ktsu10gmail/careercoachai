**Job Title:** Stress Test Engineer

**Job Attributes:**

* Salary Range: $120,000 - $180,000 per year
* Work Mode: Full-time
* Location: Remote with occasional on-site visits at our headquarters in Silicon Valley, CA
* Employment Type: Contract (6-12 months)
* Target Start Date: ASAP
* Travel: 10% travel required for on-site visits and industry conferences
* Sponsorship: Provided by the client
* Security Clearance: Top Secret clearance required

**Job Description:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer to join our team of experts in simulating real-world scenarios to test the performance, reliability, and scalability of complex systems. As a Stress Test Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and executing comprehensive stress testing plans to identify potential bottlenecks and areas for improvement.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and develop stress testing plans to simulate various types of loads, including metadata-heavy workloads
* Develop and maintain test scripts using industry-standard tools such as Apache JMeter, Gatling, or Locust
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, product management, and operations, to understand system requirements and identify areas for improvement
* Execute stress tests on production systems, identifying performance bottlenecks and areas for optimization
* Analyze test results, providing actionable insights and recommendations for system improvements
* Develop and maintain documentation of testing methodologies, scripts, and results
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies, applying knowledge to improve testing methodologies

**Required Skills:**

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
* 5+ years of experience in stress testing, performance engineering, or a related field
* Strong understanding of system architecture, scalability, and reliability principles
* Proficiency in industry-standard tools such as Apache JMeter, Gatling, or Locust
* Experience with cloud-based systems and containerization technologies (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes)
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with ability to interpret complex data sets
* Strong communication and collaboration skills, with ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams
