Responsibilities:

* Install, configure, and maintain local area networks (LANs), wide area networks (WANs), and the Internet; ensure network security and integrity
* Design, implement, and manage computer systems, including servers, workstations, and mobile devices; troubleshoot hardware and software issues
* Develop and implement backup and disaster recovery plans to minimize downtime and data loss
* Collaborate with IT teams to resolve technical issues and improve system performance
* Monitor network traffic and system logs to identify potential security threats and optimize system resources
* Implement and enforce organizational policies for computer systems, including access controls and data encryption

Required Skills:

* 3+ years of experience in network administration and/or computer systems management
* CompTIA Network+ or equivalent certification; CCNA or equivalent certification preferred
* Strong knowledge of Windows Server operating systems (Windows Server 2012 or later) and Linux operating systems (Red Hat Enterprise Linux or Ubuntu)
* Experience with network protocols, including TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, and HTTP/HTTPS
* Familiarity with virtualization technologies, such as VMware or Hyper-V
* Basic understanding of cloud computing platforms, including AWS or Azure

Preferred Skills:

* Experience with network security tools, including firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and antivirus software
* Knowledge of scripting languages, such as PowerShell or Bash
* Certification in IT service management frameworks, such as ITIL
* Experience with containerization technologies, such as Docker
* Familiarity with cybersecurity frameworks, such as NIST Cybersecurity Framework
