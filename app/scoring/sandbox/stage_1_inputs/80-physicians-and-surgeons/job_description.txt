Responsibilities:

* Diagnose and treat patients with various medical conditions
* Order and interpret diagnostic tests such as lab work, imaging studies, and other procedures
* Develop and implement treatment plans, including prescribing medications and performing surgeries
* Provide patient education and counseling to promote health and wellness
* Collaborate with other healthcare professionals, such as nurses and specialists, to coordinate care
* Stay current with the latest medical research and advancements through ongoing education and training

Required Skills:

* A Doctor of Medicine (M.D.) or Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine (D.O.) degree from an accredited institution
* Completion of a residency program in their chosen specialty
* Licensure to practice medicine in their state
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
* Proficiency in electronic medical records (EMRs) systems, such as Epic or Cerner

Preferred Skills:

* Experience with advanced medical procedures, such as minimally invasive surgery or interventional radiology
* Familiarity with medical billing and coding systems, such as ICD-10-CM
* Knowledge of health information technology (HIT) concepts, including data analytics and cybersecurity
* Certification in a specific area of medicine, such as cardiology or oncology
* Bilingual or multilingual skills
