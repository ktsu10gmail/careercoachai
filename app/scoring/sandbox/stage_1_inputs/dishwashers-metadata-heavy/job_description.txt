**Job Attributes:**

* Salary Range: $120,000 - $180,000 per year
* Work Mode: Full-time, on-site
* Location: San Francisco, CA (with occasional remote work)
* Employment Type: Permanent
* Target Start Date: 6 weeks from posting date
* Travel: Occasional travel to industry conferences and customer sites (up to 20% of the time)

* Security Clearance: Top Secret clearance required

**Job Description:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Stress Test Engineer - Scoring Engine to join our team of experts in evaluating the performance and reliability of complex scoring engines. As a key member of our Quality Assurance team, you will be responsible for designing, executing, and analyzing stress tests to identify potential issues and ensure the scalability and stability of our scoring engine.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design and execute comprehensive stress tests on our scoring engine, including load testing, performance benchmarking, and reliability analysis
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, product management, and operations, to understand system requirements and identify areas for improvement
* Develop and maintain test scripts, tools, and frameworks to support stress testing efforts
* Analyze test results, identify trends and patterns, and provide actionable recommendations for improving system performance and reliability
* Work closely with the development team to reproduce and resolve issues identified during stress testing
* Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies, and apply this knowledge to improve our scoring engine's performance and scalability

**Required Skills:**

* 5+ years of experience in stress testing or performance engineering, preferably in a metadata-heavy environment
* In-depth knowledge of scoring engines, including data processing, caching, and query optimization
* Strong understanding of computer systems, networking protocols, and database architectures
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
* Experience with test automation frameworks, such as Apache JMeter or Gatling
* Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to identify complex issues and develop effective solutions
* Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams.
