Responsibilities:

* Dispense medications to patients under the supervision of a licensed physician
* Conduct patient assessments to identify medication needs and potential interactions
* Review prescriptions for accuracy and completeness before filling
* Monitor patient responses to medications and adjust treatment plans as needed
* Maintain accurate records of patient medication use, including refill requests and adverse reactions
* Collaborate with healthcare teams to coordinate care and ensure seamless transitions between providers
* Stay current with new medications, treatments, and guidelines through ongoing education and training

Required Skills:

* Bachelor's degree in pharmacy or related field
* Current licensure as a pharmacist in the state of practice
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
* Proficiency in electronic health records (EHRs) and other clinical software
* Basic life support certification

Preferred Skills:

* Experience with automated dispensing systems (ADS)
* Familiarity with pharmacy automation software, such as Meditech or Epic Systems
* Certification in pain management or geriatric care
* Bilingual or multilingual skills
* Experience with population health management and disease prevention programs
