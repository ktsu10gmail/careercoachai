Responsibilities:

* Prepare dental instruments and equipment for patient use
* Assist dentists with procedures such as fillings, extractions, and impressions
* Take and develop radiographs (x-rays) using digital or film technology
* Measure and mix dental materials such as composite resin and cement
* Maintain accurate records of patient information, treatment plans, and billing data
* Clean and disinfect dental equipment and workstations
* Restock supplies and materials as needed
* Communicate effectively with patients, dentists, and other healthcare professionals

Required Skills:

* High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's degree in dental assisting preferred
* Completion of a state-approved dental assisting program
* Current certification as a dental assistant (CDA) through the Dental Assisting National Board (DANB)
* Basic life support (BLS) certification
* Ability to lift 50 pounds and stand for long periods

Preferred Skills:

* Experience working in a dental office or related healthcare field
* Proficiency in electronic medical records (EMRs) and practice management software
* Knowledge of dental materials, instruments, and equipment
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills
